About Me

Here are some of my favorite things

Hello! My name is Jane Boggess. I was born in NYC but I have lived most of my life in the states of Florida and Utah. They are beautiful states. In Florida some of my favorite things to do are go scuba diving, go surfing, visit the Everglades and play volleyball on the beach.


In Utah I love to go up the canyon and eat s'mores. S'mores are one of my top favorite treats! I also really enjoy visiting the many national parks here such as: Arches, Zion, Captial Reef, and Bryce Canyon!

My favorite city in the whole world in London. I love England I feel like there is just so much to in London, so everyone can have an enjoyable time. I visited with my family once and then I went on a study abroad there as well.
