This chart shows how profits are fairing in each of the 50 states in the USA.

I was interested to know why New York was one of the highest profit states. I made a chart to find out a little bit more. This chart shows how New York fared over the last 2 years by month.

I wanted to know what was selling best so I organized the data into 3 sections; furniture, office supplies, and technology. This chart shows how the profits are for each of those categories in the state of New York.

Based on the previous chart, I could see that technology brought in the most profit. I made yet another chart to forcast what the trend might look like until November of 2015. Looks pretty good!
